Data Pipeline with Apache Kafka

event 2018-06-16 (Day 1) ~ 17:40 - 18:10
explore Conference Hall 4-5
comment English
network_check Intermediate

The rise of the Apache Kafka starts a new generation of data pipeline - the stream-processing pipeline.

In this talk, Dr. Mole Wong will walk you through the concept of the stream-processing data pipeline, and how this data pipeline can be set up. He will also discuss the use cases of such a data pipeline.

Mole Wong

HK01 / Hong Kong

Dr. Mole T.Y. WONG is Data Engineer Manager in HK01 Company Limited. He is a leader of the data team in HK01. His roles include leading the data research projects, productising data science projects, as well as architecting the data pipeline in HK01.

Before joining the industry, Mole is in Senior Lecturer in CUHK, teaching various programming courses for 9 years. He is known for his passion in teaching as well as his dedication in delivering high-quality lectures, and fun is included.
